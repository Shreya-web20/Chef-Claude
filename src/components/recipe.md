      <h3>White Sauce Pasta </h3>
      <strong>Ingredients: </strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Pasta</li>
        <li>bell peppers, carrots, boiled sweet corn, broccoli</li>
        <li>Garlic</li>
        <li>Butter</li>
        <li>Flour</li>
        <li>Milk</li>
        <li>Heavy Cream</li>
        <li>Cheese</li>
        <li> Oregano, red chili flakes, freshly ground black pepper, salt </li>
      </ul>
      <strong>Instructions: </strong>
      <ol>
        <li>In a large pot, bring 3 cups of water to a boil. Then stir in some salt.</li>
        <li>Add 1 cup of dry penne pasta to the water, and stir a few times to prevent the pasta from sticking together.</li>
        <li>Cook the pasta according to the package instructions (about 10-12 minutes), occasionally stirring, until al dente or softer according to your desired texture. </li>
        <li>Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some starchy water for the sauce and drain the pasta in a colander over the sink. Set the cooked pasta aside.</li>
        <li>Heat ½ tablespoon butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic and saute until fragrant for about 30 seconds.</li>
        <li>Then add mixed veggies like ¼ cup carrot, ¼ cup bell pepper, ¼ cup broccoli, ¼ cup sweet corn, and salt. Cook the veggies till they are tender but still crunchy, for around 2 minutes. Turn off the flame and keep it aside.</li>
        <li>Melt 1.5 tablespoons butter in a saucepan over low heat. Add 1.5 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida), and stir continuously until well combined and smooth. Cook for a minute until the raw smell of the flour goes away. Do not over-roast the flour, and ensure the color of the flour doesn't change.</li>
        <li>Slowly add in 1.5 cups whole milk and stir continuously with a whisk until no lumps are left. Then add the remaining milk and whisk to combine.</li>
        <li>Cook it for about 4-5 minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce boils and thickens.</li>
        <li>Once the sauce is slightly thick, add 2 tablespoons heavy cream, 2-3 tablespoons cheese (mozzarella), ½ teaspoon dried oregano, ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes, black pepper, and salt.</li>
        <li>Continue stirring until the cheese has melted. When the sauce coats the back of the spoon, then it is done. The sauce will thicken as it cools.</li>
        <li>Add the sauteed veggies and the cooked penne pasta to the sauce. Toss well to coat the pasta with creamy white sauce.</li>
        <li>Lastly, transfer it to a serving plate, sprinkle some cheese and red chili flakes, if desired, and serve it immediately Enjoy!</li>
      </ol>
